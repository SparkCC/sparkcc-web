SparkCC is a community of makers on the NSW Central Coast

Our non-government, not-for-profit community is centred on our purpose-built makerspace in Somersby where members have access to a wide variety of workspaces and equipment. We work individually and collectively on all manner of interesting projects, learning from one another and having a lot of fun along the way.

Think of us as a social club for people who like to make things!

What We Do

What We Do

Just some of what you'll find happening at SparkCC:

Electronics

Our electronics bench has everything you need to work with circuits and microcontrollers.

CAD

Dream it and then design it on our high-end workstation equipped with various CAD packages.

Code

Be you Newbie or Ninja you'll find programming mentors and collaborators at SparkCC.

3D Printing

Use our 3D print farm to make your designs a reality! Current printer count: 6.

CNC Routing

Build BIG projects on our full-sheet CNC routing table.

Laser Cutting

Our huge 130W laser cutter cuts & etches acrylic, particle board & timber.

E-Vehicles

From e-bikes to hybrid and all-electric cars, Sparkers vehicles with batteries.

Workshops

Join a workshop and learn a new skill. Or share your expertise by conducting one yourself!
